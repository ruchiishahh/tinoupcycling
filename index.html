Activities

We make a variety of projects and crafts at meetings.

Re-using Waste

We re-use discarded materials and create projects & crafts out of them to sell. This money is then donated to various charities.

Spread Awarness

One of our main goals is to tell more people about upcycling in order to decrease waste in our community.

Enhance School

We make projects and display them all over the school to add not only beauty, but also creativity to the school.

About

The series of events that have shaped Tino Upcycling.

  • December 2013

    Our Beginnings

    Brainstormed idea for starting Tino Upcycling for the first time and began upcycling.

  • April 2014

    Getting Started

    After raising money by selling upcycled jewelry, donated first $1000 to KIVA and AAUW.

  • May 2014

    Bigger Connections

    Made our first appearance at Maker Faire in order to spread awarness about upcycling and learn from others

  • August 2016

    First Meeting

    Tino Upcycling members and officers had their first meeting and made various projects and crafts.
